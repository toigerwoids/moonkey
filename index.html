The Legend of Cosmo

It began in a jungle clearing under the stars. While other monkeys swung through vines and flung fruit for fun, Cosmo stared at the sky.

He wasn't born on the ground. At least, he didn't feel like he was. From the moment he opened his eyes, he knew there was something waiting above.

Every night, Cosmo would point to the moon with a peeled banana and whisper, "I'm coming for you."

The tribe thought he was bananas. They mocked his training: vine climbs in zero gravity drills, mud-suit simulations, coconut helmet stress tests. But Cosmo didn't care.

He had a mission.

The Launch Heard Around the Trees

One night, the jungle fell silent. A burst of flame lit the canopy. Cosmo had launched himself into space using parts from an old airplane wreckage.

All that remained was a clearing, a crater, and a scribbled note: "Be back never. Save some bananas."

No one saw him again — but soon, the stars above began to shift. On full moons, some say you can see a tiny figure orbiting in loops. Others say Cosmo became the moon.

We say — he became the message. Moonkey was born to honor his legacy. A token powered by the spirit of one monkey… who aimed higher.
